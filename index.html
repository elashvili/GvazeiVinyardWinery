Our Heritage

Born from
the ancient vine

Gvazei Winery is rooted in Georgia's Kakheti region — the cradle of wine civilization, where qvevri winemaking was born over 8,000 years ago. We carry that tradition forward with care and without compromise.

Our wines are unfiltered, alive, and made with minimal intervention — honoring the soil, the grape, and the season. No additives. No shortcuts. Pure wine as nature intended.
